Build a full-stack web app called CarryOn™ — a secure estate planning and legacy management platform. The application should feature three distinct portals: one for the benefactor (estate owner), one for beneficiaries, and an admin portal for platform management.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
-   **Authentication:** Secure login with email, password, and 6-digit OTP 2FA (includes email and SMS).

-   **Messaging:** A text-based messaging feature for users to communicate with the CarryOn customer support team.
-   **Push Notifications:** PWA-based push notifications for important events (new messages, status changes, etc.).
