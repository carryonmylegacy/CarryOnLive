<analysis>**original_problem_statement:**
Build a full-stack web app called CarryOn™ — a secure estate planning and legacy management platform. There is NO separate role selector page; users start as the benefactor (estate owner) and prepare everything from one portal. After death, designated beneficiaries gain full access via a transition triggered by uploading a death certificate.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
-   **Authentication:** Secure login with email, password, and 6-digit OTP 2FA. User creation flow.
-   **Benefactor Portal:**
    -   Dashboard with readiness gauge and checklists.
    -   Secure Document Vault with categorized uploads, locking (password/voice/backup key), and encryption (AES-256).
    -   Milestone Messages (text/video) with triggers.
    -   Beneficiary Manager to add/edit family members.
    -   Estate Guardian AI chat for planning advice.
    -   Checklists for immediate actions.
    -   Mock Trustee Services section.
    -   Settings with theme toggle and profile.
-   **Transition Mechanism:** Secure section to upload a death certificate, which, after a mock approval, transitions the estate status and unlocks the beneficiary view.
-   **Beneficiary View:**
    -   Limited pre-transition view.
    -   Full access post-transition to view documents, messages, and report milestones.
-   **Technical Stack:** React frontend (with Tailwind/shadcn), FastAPI backend, and MongoDB database.
-   **User Request (In Progress):** Implement a detailed algorithm to calculate the Estate Readiness Score based on specific criteria for documents, milestone messages, and a checklist.
    -   **Documents (100%):** Requires 1 will, 1 trust, Financial Power of Attorney, Medical Power of Attorney, and a Healthcare Directive/Living Will. Names must match user-provided info.
    -   **Milestone Messages (100%):** Requires one immediate message post-death, plus messages for major life milestones for each beneficiary based on age and demographics (e.g., graduations, marriage, first child for a young person; retirement for a spouse).
    -   **Checklist (100%):** Requires no less than 25 items spanning from immediate actions to tasks within a month after death.
