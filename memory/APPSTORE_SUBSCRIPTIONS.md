# App Store Connect — Subscription Products Setup Guide (v2 IDs)

## Prerequisites
1. Go to App Store Connect → Your App → Subscriptions
2. Create 2 Subscription Groups:
   - **CarryOn Benefactor**
   - **CarryOn Beneficiary**

## Notes
- Hospice Benefactor is FREE (no IAP needed)
- Enterprise/B2B is FREE (no IAP needed)
- All Product IDs use `.v2.` prefix to avoid conflicts with previously deleted IDs
