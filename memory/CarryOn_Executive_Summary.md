CarryOn™ Enterprises Inc — Executive Summary

PROBLEM: When a casualty occurs — unorganized estate documents, unclear wishes, and incomplete posthumous instructions create immediate chaos for survivors, diverting operational focus from warfighting. This distraction ripples through commands consuming time and diverting resources from the mission to administrative support, grief management, and resultant family crises.

SOLUTION: CarryOn™ is a secure, AI-powered family readiness platform enabling Sailors to:

  • Organize estate documents in a zero-knowledge encrypted vault (AES-256-GCM, per-user derived encryption keys).

  • Auto-generate Immediate Action Checklist items via Estate Guardian™ — an AI analyst that reviews vault contents against U.S. estate law across all 50 states and territories, identifying gaps, contradictions, and missing provisions. All documents remain encrypted at rest; the AI operates within the platform's secure boundary and no human accesses user data.

  • Record Milestone Messages (text, voice, or video) for automatic delivery to beneficiaries at key life events — weddings, graduations, births, and any user-defined milestone.

  • Authorize Designated Trustee Services for confidential post-transition task execution (e.g., account closures, credential destruction, financial transfers).

DoN-ready features include a Commander Family Readiness Oversight Tool offering aggregated, anonymized unit-level visibility into individual Sailor family preparedness status — preserving privacy while enabling fast verification and slashing administrative burden on operational units.

KEY BENEFITS:

  • Reduced Operational Distractions: Preempts posthumous family crises by automating family readiness workflows, allowing units to stay mission-focused during high-stakes periods; Sailors and leaders deploy with peace of mind, knowing Navy families have clear, executable plans.

  • Enhanced Readiness and Retention: Boosts morale and resilience with tools like Milestone Messages and AI-powered estate analysis; tailored for military life with verified military/first responder pricing.

  • Scalable Command Oversight: Aggregates completion metrics (e.g., vaults ready, checklists active) for pre-deployment checks, helping Commanders confirm unit family preparedness at scale.

  • Compliance and Security: AES-256 zero-knowledge encryption, SOC 2 and GDPR compliance architecture in place (formal certification pending); platform built with input from retired Senior Navy leaders and Navy Spouses.

POTENTIAL COST: Near-zero upfront — commercial platform available to active-duty Service Members at a flat $5.99/mo/benefactor ($1.99/mo/beneficiary); scalable for GovCon bulk provisioning as a Service Member benefit at further discounted or no per-user cost. Customizable to include additional CNP family readiness priorities.
